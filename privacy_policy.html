Privacy Policy

Last Updated: October 26, 2023

Welcome to Contact Translator. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our application handles your information, specifically regarding your contacts and data processing.

1. Information We Access

To provide its core functionality, Contact Translator requires the following permissions:

  • Contacts (Read/Write): We access your contact list to identify names that require translation and to update those names with the translated versions.
  • Storage Access: The app requires permission to save and read backup files (JSON) that you manually create to safeguard your original contact names.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use the data accessed from your contacts solely for the following purposes:

  • Translation: Converting contact names from English to your selected language using phonetic or semantic algorithms.
  • Safety/Backups: Allowing you to create a local backup file of your contacts before any changes are made, so you can restore them at any time.

3. Data Processing and Third Parties

We prioritize on-device processing to keep your data private. However, depending on the translation mode you choose, data may be processed as follows:

  • On-Device (ML Kit): Most translations are performed locally on your device using Google ML Kit. In this mode, your contact data does not leave your device.
  • Online Fallback: If the local model is unavailable or if a specific language requires it, the app may send contact names (text only) to Google's translation servers. This is done securely via HTTPS.
  • No Data Collection: We do not have our own servers. We do not collect, store, or sell your personal information or contact list.

4. Data Retention and Deletion

Because Contact Translator does not use external servers to store your information, your data remains entirely under your control. We provide the following ways for you to manage and delete your data:

  • Reverting Translations: You can use the "Remove All Translation from Contacts" button within the app to remove translated names and restore your contacts to their original state.
  • Deleting Backups: Any backup files (JSON) you create are stored in the location you chose on your device. You can delete these files at any time using any File Manager app.
  • App Data: You can clear all app-related settings by going to your Android Settings > Apps > Contact Translator > Storage > Clear Data.

If you have any questions about how to delete your data, you can contact us at the email address provided below, and we will guide you through the process.

5. Security

We implement industry-standard security measures within the app to protect your data. Since all updates happen locally on your phone, the security of your contacts is also protected by your Android system's security features.

6. Children’s Privacy

Contact Translator does not knowingly collect any personal information from children. Our app is a utility tool intended for general audiences.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.
